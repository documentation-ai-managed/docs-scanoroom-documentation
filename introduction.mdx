## Getting Started

ScanoRoom transforms your iPhone into a professional 3D scanner, enabling you to create detailed floor plans and 3D visualizations of spaces. This innovative solution captures environments with precision, processes the data into usable models, and allows for easy export to various formats. Whether you're an architect, real estate professional, or DIY enthusiast, ScanoRoom provides the tools to digitize physical spaces efficiently.

## How It Works

The scanning process combines your iPhone's advanced sensors with ScanoRoom's processing algorithms to create detailed 3D models. You simply walk around a space while the app captures data points, which are then stitched together into a cohesive model.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="LiDAR Technology" default-open="true">
    Modern iPhones equipped with LiDAR sensors can capture depth information with millimeter precision. This technology enables accurate measurements without additional hardware.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Point Cloud Processing" default-open="false">
    Raw sensor data is processed into organized point clouds, which represent the spatial coordinates of captured surfaces.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Mesh Generation" default-open="false">
    Point clouds are converted into smooth 3D meshes that can be viewed, measured, and exported.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## System Requirements

ScanoRoom requires an iPhone with LiDAR capabilities, including iPhone 12 Pro and later models. The app is compatible with iOS 15.0 or higher and requires approximately 500MB of free storage space.
