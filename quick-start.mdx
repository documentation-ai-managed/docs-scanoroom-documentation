## Installation and Setup

Begin your ScanoRoom journey by downloading the app from the App Store and completing the initial setup. The process takes less than five minutes and requires no special configuration.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download App" icon="download">
    Search for "ScanoRoom" in the App Store and install the latest version on your compatible iPhone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant Permissions" icon="shield">
    Allow camera and location permissions when prompted to enable full scanning functionality.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up for a free account to access cloud storage and advanced features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Calibrate Device" icon="settings">
    Follow the on-screen calibration process to optimize scanning accuracy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your First Scan

Once setup is complete, you can immediately start scanning. ScanoRoom guides you through the entire process with visual cues and audio feedback.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Indoor Spaces" icon="home" href="#indoor-scanning">
    Perfect for rooms, offices, and apartments up to 100 square meters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Outdoor Areas" icon="tree" href="#outdoor-scanning">
    Capture gardens, facades, and exterior spaces with proper lighting.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Step-by-Step Guide" icon="list">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Project" icon="folder-plus">
        Create a new project or open an existing one from the main menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start Scanning" icon="play-circle">
        Tap the scan button and begin moving your iPhone slowly around the space.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Monitor Progress" icon="bar-chart">
        Watch the progress indicator as the app captures and processes data.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete Scan" icon="check-circle">
        Stop scanning when you've covered the entire area of interest.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Best Practices" icon="thumbs-up">
    <Callout kind="tip">
      Move at a consistent speed of about 1 meter per second for optimal data capture.
    </Callout>
    
    <Expandable title="Lighting Tips" default-open="false">
      Ensure adequate lighting but avoid direct sunlight that can cause sensor interference.
    </Expandable>
    
    <Expandable title="Movement Patterns" default-open="false">
      Use a systematic scanning pattern, overlapping each pass by 20-30% for complete coverage.
    </Expandable>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Reviewing Your Results

After completing a scan, ScanoRoom automatically processes the data into a viewable 3D model. You can immediately see measurements and explore the captured space.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="3D View Navigation" default-open="true">
    Use two-finger gestures to rotate, zoom, and pan through your 3D model. Pinch to zoom and swipe to change viewing angle.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Measurement Tools" default-open="false">
    Tap any two points to measure distances, areas, and angles within your scan.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Annotation Features" default-open="false">
    Add text notes, arrows, and highlights to mark important features or measurements.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Saving and Sharing

ScanoRoom automatically saves your projects to the cloud, allowing access from multiple devices and easy sharing with colleagues.

<CodeGroup tabs="Share Options,Export Settings">
````javascript
// Share project with team
ScanoRoom.share({
  projectId: "scan_123",
  recipients: ["colleague@company.com"],
  permissions: "view"
});
````
````javascript
// Configure auto-save settings
ScanoRoom.settings({
  autoSave: true,
  frequency: "every_30_seconds",
  cloudBackup: true
});
````
</CodeGroup>

<Callout kind="info">
  Projects are automatically backed up to secure cloud storage with end-to-end encryption.
</Callout>
